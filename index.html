Y.R Dry Fish is a Mumbai-based dry fish brand known for delivering fresh, high-quality dried seafood across Maharashtra. By sourcing directly from trusted fishermen and ensuring careful drying and packaging, we bridge the gap between coastal authenticity and modern convenience, bringing the taste of the sea to urban homes.
